INSTANT CONTEXT SIDEBAR - DEMO GUIDE
======================================

SETUP (One-Time)
----------------
1. Open Chrome > go to chrome://extensions
2. Enable "Developer mode" (top-right toggle)
3. Click "Load unpacked" and select the "instant-context-sidebar" folder
4. The extension icon appears in the toolbar

DEMO WALKTHROUGH
----------------

1. OPEN THE SIDEBAR
   - Navigate to any content-rich webpage (e.g., a Wikipedia article or blog post)
   - Notice the purple bolt icon (FAB) pulsing in the bottom-right corner
   - Click it to open the sidebar (or press Ctrl+Shift+S)
   - The sidebar slides in from the right with a smooth animation

2. SUMMARY TAB
   - The extension auto-analyzes the page on open
   - The Summary tab shows a concise 2-4 sentence "What this page is really about" explainer
   - Highlighted with an accent bar for quick scanning

3. DEFINITIONS TAB
   - Click the "Definitions" tab
   - Shows up to 8 key terms or jargon found on the page
   - Each term has a clear, plain-language definition
   - Displayed as numbered cards with hover effects

4. LINKS TAB
   - Click the "Links" tab
   - "Suggested Topics" - AI-generated topics for deeper exploration (opens Google search)
   - "Links on This Page" - Extracted links from the actual page content

5. HISTORY TAB
   - Click the "History" tab
   - Shows previously analyzed pages (up to 30)
   - Each card displays: AI-generated keyword tag, summary snippet, hostname, timestamp
   - Click any card to revisit that page
   - "Clear History" button at the top to reset

6. THEME TOGGLE
   - Click the moon/sun icon in the header to switch between light and dark themes
   - Theme preference persists across sessions

7. RE-ANALYZE
   - Click the refresh button at the bottom of the sidebar to re-analyze the current page

8. CLOSE THE SIDEBAR
   - Click the X button in the header, the FAB icon, or press Ctrl+Shift+S again

KEY FEATURES TO HIGHLIGHT
-------------------------
- Works on ANY webpage
- AI-powered analysis using Azure OpenAI (GPT)
- Four distinct tabs: Summary, Definitions, Links, History
- Light/dark theme with persistence
- Keyboard shortcut support (Ctrl+Shift+S / Cmd+Shift+S)
- Smooth animations and polished UI
- Cross-browser: works on Chrome and Firefox
- No frameworks - pure vanilla JavaScript and CSS
